History

Founded in 2015 in Austin, Texas, Tech Con began as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of North America's leading technology conferences. Over nine years, we've hosted over 50,000 attendees and 1,500 speakers, showcasing innovations from startups to tech giants. In 2024, we celebrate our 10th anniversary with an exciting hybrid format.

Mission

To inspire, educate, and connect the global technology community by showcasing innovation, fostering collaboration, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders. We focus on highlighting breakthrough technologies, creating inclusive spaces for all technologists, and building meaningful connections that drive the industry forward.
